About

Innovating Godda is a community with an ambition to glorify Godda.

We conduct regular events like Tasveer, Monthly Quizzes, Seminars, Guest Lectures, and multiple events on a regular basis. To enhance the proficiency of the people Innovating Godda is looking forward for conducting workshops and performs career counselling.

We work with a vision to boost Godda by enriching its people in an innovative manner. In the near future, we intend to organize an annual techno-cultural fest.
